Data Deletion Request

This page is for users of the mobile game Tic Tac Toe Modern who wish to request deletion of their personal data.

What data we collect:

  • Google account ID (UID)
  • Name
  • Email address

How to request data deletion:

Please email your request to: mohsanriaz1987@gmail.com

Your email should include:

  • The Google account email you used to sign in
  • Your display name (if different)

What happens next:

  • We will delete all personal data stored in our system related to your account
  • You will still retain your Google account – we do not delete or alter your Google credentials
  • All data will be deleted within 7 business days

If you would like to delete your entire account and game access, please mention that in your email request.
